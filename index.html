Child Safety Standards – CSAE Policy

At Hinmatch, we are committed to protecting children from child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our Safety Standards:

  • We strictly prohibit any content related to Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) on our platform.
  • We implement in-app reporting tools so users can easily report concerns related to child safety.
  • We comply with all applicable child protection laws and cooperate with regional and national authorities where necessary.
  • We continuously train our team on CSAE compliance, reporting, and detection.

Contact Information:

If you have any concerns regarding child safety or want to report a violation, please contact us at: hindumatching@gmail.com

Legal Compliance:

This policy complies with Google Play's Child Safety Standards and global best practices. We are dedicated to ensuring the safety and well-being of minors across all areas of our application.
